Calculated Columns and Measures 10

Now that the Data has been loaded, Date and Measures tables have been created, it's time for the DAX Functions. 

**You can either copy and paste the functions below into the file you created or just download the pbix file  that already has the functions done. Click the icon  above


Calculated Column:  start by creating this calculated column as shown below


Revenue = Avocado[AveragePrice] * Avocado[Total Volume]


Calculated Measures: Create the following measures in the newly created Avo Measures Table. Follows the instructions below for all the measures



1. Avg. Price = AVERAGE(Avocado[AveragePrice])

2. Volume = SUM(Avocado[Total Volume])

3. Revenue = (SUM(Avocado[Revenue]))


4. AVG Price LY = CALCULATE(AVERAGE(Avocado[AveragePrice]),SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(DateTable[Date]),ALLSELECTED(DateTable[Date]))

5. Avg. Price LY Var = IF(ISBLANK('Avo Measures'[AVG Price LY]),BLANK(),'Avo Measures'[Avg. Price]- 'Avo Measures'[AVG Price LY])

6. YOY Price = DIVIDE('Avo Measures'[Avg. Price LY Var],'Avo Measures'[AVG Price LY])


7. Volume LY = CALCULATE('Avo Measures'[Volume],SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(DateTable[Date]))

8. Volume LY Var = IF(ISBLANK('Avo Measures'[Volume LY]),BLANK(),'Avo Measures'[Volume] - 'Avo Measures'[Volume LY])

9. YOY Volume = DIVIDE('Avo Measures'[Volume LY Var],'Avo Measures'[Volume LY])


10. Revenue LY = CALCULATE('Avo Measures'[Revenue],SAMEPERIODLASTYEAR(DateTable[Date]))

11. Revenue LY Var = IF(ISBLANK('Avo Measures'[Revenue LY]),BLANK(),'Avo Measures'[Revenue] - 'Avo Measures'[Revenue LY])

12. YOY Revenue = DIVIDE('Avo Measures'[Revenue LY Var],'Avo Measures'[Revenue LY])

The following measure are for the Arrows

KPI AVG Arrow = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Avg. Price] > [AVG Price LY], UNICHAR(9650),UNICHAR(9660))

KPI AVG Color = IF([KPI AVG Arrow] = UNICHAR(9650), "#009900", "#FF0000")

KPI Rev Arrow = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Revenue] > [Revenue LY], UNICHAR(9650),UNICHAR(9660))

KPI Rev Color = IF([KPI Rev Arrow] = UNICHAR(9650), "#009900", "#FF0000")

KPI Volume Arrow = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Volume] > [Volume LY], UNICHAR(9650),UNICHAR(9660))

KPI Volume Color = IF([KPI Volume Arrow] = UNICHAR(9650), "#009900", "#FF0000")

The following measure are for the YOY Color Conditional formatting

YOY Price KPI = SWITCH(TRUE(), [Avg. Price] > [AVG Price LY], "#009900", "#FF0000")

YOY Revenue KPI = SWITCH(TRUE(), 'Avo Measures'[Revenue] > 'Avo Measures'[Revenue LY], "#009900", "#FF0000")

YOY Volume KPI = SWITCH(TRUE(), 'Avo Measures'[Volume] > 'Avo Measures'[Volume LY], "#009900", "#FF0000")
